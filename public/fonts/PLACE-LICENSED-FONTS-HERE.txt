Drop the licensed commercial webfonts into this directory:

  public/fonts/PPEditorialNew-Regular.woff2
  public/fonts/PPEditorialNew-Italic.woff2
  public/fonts/Sohne-Buch.woff2
  public/fonts/Sohne-Kraftig.woff2
  public/fonts/SohneMono-Buch.woff2

Then open app/layout.tsx and swap the `next/font/google` imports for
`next/font/local`, pointing at the files above. That is the only code
change needed — tailwind.config.ts already prefers the licensed family
name first and falls back to the Google-hosted stand-in automatically.

Purchase sources:
  - PP Editorial New: https://pangrampangram.com/products/editorial-new
  - Söhne:            https://klim.co.nz/retail-fonts/soehne/
